A Page, About Me...

I am a self-taught web developer located in Moline, Illinois (aka: The Quad Cities). I originally started my career in the information technology field in 2007.

I have served in many different IT roles since then, including enlisting in the Army and serving a 3 years in Germany.

After my time in the military, I returned to the US and decided on a new career. Thus, came learning web develpment.

The creativity, complexity, and all the challenges that it encompasses it, makes it an amazing career field to be in.

In my spare time I enjoy spending time with my wife, Heather, my 3 year old son, and our recent new addition to the family, a little girl born this past June!

When I'm not spending time with loved ones I enjoy catching up on friends from around the world, reading, playing video games, and creating my own web apps.

If you are looking for a new website or thinking about upgrading an existing one, please contact me!

Thank you! ~Brian
