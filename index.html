A Page, About Me...

I am a self-taught web developer located in Moline, Illinois (aka: The Quad Cities). I originally got started in the information technology field in 2007.

I have served in many different roles since then, including an overseas tour in Germany while serving in the Army.

After my military contract ended I returned to the US and really wanted a career change.

Thus, came web develpment. I love web development and the creativity, complexity of problems, and everything else that it encompasses.

In my spare time I enjoy spending time with my lovely wife, Heather and my 3 year old son. We also have a little girl on the way due in June, which is a very exciting!

If you are looking for a new website or thinking about upgrading an existing one, please contact me and lets talk about it!

Thank you! ~Brian
